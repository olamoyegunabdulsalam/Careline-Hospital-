About Us

Compassionate care for every stage of your health journey

At Careline, we believe everyone deserves access to exceptional healthcare in a warm, welcoming environment. Founded in 2010 by Dr. Sarah Mitchell, our clinic began as a small practice with a big mission: to treat patients like family.

Over the past decade, we've grown into a multispecialty healthcare center serving over 50,000 patients. From routine checkups to complex treatments, our team combines cutting-edge technology with old-fashioned empathy.

Our Core Values:

  • Patient-Centered Care: Tailored treatment plans for every individual
  • Community Focus: Free health camps and educational workshops
  • Innovation: Paperless records and telemedicine for convenience
